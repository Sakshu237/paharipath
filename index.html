Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Applies to paharipath.in and the PahariPath platform

1. Who we are
PahariPath ("we", "us") operates a travel discovery and booking platform for Himachal Pradesh, India. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and the rights you have over it under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

2. What we collect

  • When you book a stay: name, phone number, email (optional), number of guests, check-in/check-out dates.
  • When you create a Traveller Profile: name, phone number, and activity history (bookings, eco offsets, pledges, PAHADI points).
  • When you register as a host: name, property details, phone number, email, and login credentials.
  • When you submit a Community Pin or report a location correction: the location itself, an optional description, and your name if you choose to provide it.
  • Payments: processed directly by Razorpay. We never see or store your card, UPI, or bank details — only the payment confirmation and amount.
  • Basic usage analytics via Plausible Analytics — a cookieless, privacy-first tool that does not track individuals or share data with third parties.

3. Why we collect it
To process and confirm bookings, connect you with the host you're booking, operate the PAHADI traveller rewards system, respond to enquiries, maintain the accuracy of destination and map data, and improve the platform. We do not collect more than is needed for these purposes.

4. Who we share it with

  • Razorpay (payment processing) — handles your payment details directly; governed by Razorpay's own privacy policy.
  • Your host — when you book a stay, we share your name, phone number, and booking dates with that specific host so they can prepare for your visit.
  • Supabase (our database and backend infrastructure provider) — stores platform data on our behalf under standard data-processing terms.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone, ever.

5. Cookies
We use only minimal, functional cookies (e.g. remembering your cookie consent choice). Our analytics tool, Plausible, does not use cookies at all. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Data retention
We retain booking and traveller profile data for as long as your account is active or as needed to resolve disputes, comply with tax/accounting obligations, and maintain the PAHADI points history. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

7. Children's privacy
PahariPath is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact us and we'll remove it.

8. Your rights
Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email hello@paharipath.in with your registered phone number or email.

9. Grievance Officer
As required under Indian law, grievances relating to this policy can be directed to our Grievance Officer at hello@paharipath.in. [Founder: please designate a named Grievance Officer with contact details here before public launch — required under the DPDP Act.]

10. Security
Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with access controls on our database. No system is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

11. Changes to this policy

Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

1. About PahariPath
PahariPath is a discovery and booking marketplace connecting travellers with independent local hosts across Himachal Pradesh. We are an intermediary platform — the actual accommodation contract is between you and the host, not with PahariPath.

2. Eligibility
You must be at least 18 years old to make a booking or register as a host on PahariPath.

3. Bookings & payment
Bookings are confirmed once payment is completed via Razorpay and the host has been notified. Prices shown include a 5% GST component, applied at checkout. Cancellations made more than 48 hours before check-in are eligible for a free cancellation; cancellation terms within 48 hours of check-in are at the host's discretion — contact the host directly via the number provided in your booking confirmation.

4. Carbon offset contributions
Carbon offset amounts added at checkout are voluntary contributions passed toward tree-planting initiatives via our conservation partner. They are non-refundable once a booking is confirmed.

5. Eco Permits
Eco Permits for sensitive zones (e.g. Spiti, Parvati, Pin Valley) are date-specific, non-transferable, and non-refundable once issued. 70% of the permit fee is directed to local conservation efforts.

6. PAHADI Traveller Points
PAHADI points, tiers, and badges are a loyalty and recognition system with no cash value and cannot be redeemed, transferred, or sold. We may adjust the points system, thresholds, or rewards at any time.

7. Crowd data & forecasts
Live crowd levels, weekly forecasts, and weather/road-safety guidance are estimates based on historical patterns, seasonal data, and host-submitted updates. They are indicative only, not a guarantee of actual conditions — always verify road and weather conditions independently before travelling to remote or high-altitude areas.

8. Community-submitted content
Community Pins, location corrections, and reviews are user-submitted and reviewed by our team before appearing publicly. By submitting content, you grant PahariPath a non-exclusive right to display it on the platform. We may edit, reject, or remove submissions at our discretion.

9. Host responsibilities
Hosts are responsible for the accuracy of their listings, honoring confirmed bookings, complying with applicable local, state, and tax regulations (including any Himachal Pradesh tourism/homestay registration requirements), and maintaining safe accommodations.

10. Prohibited conduct

Bookings in high-altitude or environmentally sensitive areas carry inherent risk — travel at your own discretion and judgment.
