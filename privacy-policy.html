Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25-06-2026  ·  Last Updated: 25-06-2026

PahariPath ("PahariPath," "we," "us," "our") operates paharipath.in and related mobile/web services (the "Platform"), a responsible travel discovery and booking platform for Himachal Pradesh. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal data when you use the Platform as a traveller, host/homestay partner, or visitor.

This Policy is drafted to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and DPDP Rules, 2025 ("DPDP Act"), and, where relevant to international visitors, is written to align with the core principles of the GDPR and similar frameworks. By using the Platform, you agree to the practices described here.

PahariPath (Udyam-registered), with registered address at Near GSSS, Ward No. 6, Nadaun, Distt. Hamirpur (H.P.) 177033, is the Data Fiduciary for personal data processed through the Platform.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the purpose stated. Data falls into these categories:

1.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Name, email address, phone number, password (hashed), date of birth (for age/consent verification)
  • Profile photo, home city/region (optional)

1.2 Booking & Transaction Data

  • Booking dates, destinations, homestay/property selected, number of travellers
  • Payment status, transaction ID, amount paid (via Razorpay — we do not receive, see, or store your card number, CVV, UPI ID, or net-banking credentials; these go directly to Razorpay's PCI-DSS Level 1 certified infrastructure)
  • Invoices and refund records

1.3 Location & Movement Data

  • GPS data for PAHADI tier challenges: when you opt in to GPS-verified challenges, we collect your device location only at the moment of check-in/verification — not continuous background tracking
  • Community map pins: location and content of pins you voluntarily submit
  • Crowd heatmap / forecast data: aggregated and anonymized before display; individual movement is never shown to other users
  • General IP-based location for regional content and fraud prevention

1.4 Host / Homestay Partner Data

  • Property details, address, ownership/registration documents, bank/UPI details for payouts, government ID for verification
  • Availability calendar and pricing data

1.5 Communications Data

  • Messages between travellers and hosts on the Platform, support tickets, reviews
  • Language preference for Kangri/Pahari or other regional language content

1.6 Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, browser, IP address, cookies, pages visited, session duration
  • Crash logs and performance data

1.7 Sensitive Data

The DPDP Act does not create a separate "sensitive data" category like GDPR, but we treat the following with extra care regardless: precise GPS location, government ID documents (host KYC), payment metadata, and any data belonging to a person under 18.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act, we process your data based on:

  • Your consent — freely given, specific, informed, and revocable at any time
  • Legitimate use — e.g., completing a booking you initiated, responding to your support request, or fraud prevention

Consent requests are presented in plain language, separately from Terms of Service, specifying exactly what is collected and why.

4. Children's Data

The Platform is not directed at individuals under 18. If we learn a user is under 18, we require verifiable parental/guardian consent before any further processing, and will restrict features (like GPS challenges and public map pins) until consent is confirmed. If you believe a child has created an account without such consent, contact us immediately at the grievance contact in Section 12.

5. Who We Share Data With

  • Razorpay (payment processor) — transaction amount, order reference, and your name/contact for the specific booking. PCI-DSS Level 1 certified and RBI-authorised.
  • Supabase (database/backend infrastructure) — acts as our data processor, bound by a data processing agreement.
  • Vercel (hosting) — technical/server logs necessary to serve the Platform.
  • Homestay hosts — only the booking details needed to fulfil your stay. Hosts are contractually required to use this data solely for that booking.
  • OSRM routing service — route queries, which do not require your identity.
  • Analytics providers (if used) — aggregated, de-identified usage data.
  • Government/regulatory authorities — only where legally required.
  • Business transfers — if PahariPath is acquired or merged, with notice to you.

We do not permit any third party to use PahariPath user data for their own independent marketing purposes.

6. Cross-Border Data Storage

Some infrastructure providers (Supabase, Vercel) may store or process data on servers outside India. Where this occurs, we require contractual safeguards consistent with DPDP Act requirements. Payment data follows RBI data localization rules — relevant transaction data is localized in India, and any temporary foreign copies are purged rapidly.

8. Your Rights as a Data Principal

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

  1. Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  2. Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  3. Erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  4. Withdraw consent — at any time, via account settings or by contacting us
  5. Grievance redressal — raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer; if unresolved, escalate to the Data Protection Board of India
  6. Nominate a representative — to exercise your rights on your behalf in case of death or incapacity

9. Security Safeguards

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, no storage of raw payment card data, regular review of admin/host dashboard permissions, and an incident response process for suspected breaches.

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

10. Data Breach Notification

If a personal data breach occurs that is likely to affect you, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users without undue delay, describing the nature of the breach, likely consequences, and remedial steps.

11. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies — session management, login state, and remembering your cookie consent choice
  • Functional cookies — remembering language/region preference
  • Analytics — we use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first tool that does not use cookies and does not track individual visitors across sites. It reports only aggregated, anonymous usage statistics (e.g. page views, referrers) and cannot identify you personally.

We do not use third-party advertising trackers, and we do not use cookies to build cross-site profiles of you.
